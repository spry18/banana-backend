This is a fantastic question. To make sense of everything, let’s look at the lifecycle of an Enquiry from the moment a Field Owner creates it, all the way to the point where the Driver drops off the load. 

Based on the schemas and controllers we have running in your codebase, here is the **End-to-End Enquiry & Logistics Flow**, broken down by role and the corresponding `status`.

---

### Step 1: The Field Owner (Creation)
**Actor:** Field Owner (Farmer)
**Action:** The Field Owner submits a new Enquiry indicating they have crops ready to sell.
* **Enquiry Status:** `PENDING`
* **Behind the scenes:** A new record is created in the `Enquiry` collection.

---

### Step 2: The Field Selector / Visitor (Inspection & Selection)
**Actor:** Field Visitor/Selector
**Action:** The OM assigns a Selector, or the Selector picks up a `PENDING` Enquiry, visits the farm, logs the crop quality (4H, 5H, etc.), and reports the estimated boxes.
* **Enquiry Status:** Changes from `PENDING` ➔ `SELECTED` (or `REJECTED` if the crop isn't good, or `RESCHEDULED`).
* **Behind the scenes:** The Enquiry is now waiting on the Operational Manager to fix the final rate based on the Selector's report.

---

### Step 3: The Operational Manager (Negotiation & Allocation)
**Actor:** Operational Manager (OM)
**Action 1 (Rate Fixing):** The OM reviews the `SELECTED` enquiry, negotiates the `purchaseRate` with the farmer, and locks it in.
* **Enquiry Status:** Changes from `SELECTED` ➔ `RATE_FIXED`

**Action 2 (Logistics Assignment):** Once the rate is fixed, the OM creates a Logistics record. They assign a vehicle, an Eicher Driver (Main `driverId`), and optionally a Pickup Driver (`pickupDriverId`) to the Enquiry.
* **Enquiry Status:** Changes from `RATE_FIXED` ➔ `ASSIGNED`
* **Logistics `assignmentStatus`:** Created as `PENDING`
* **Logistics `transitStatus`:** Created as `DISPATCHED`

---

### Step 4: The Driver (Transportation)
**Actor:** Eicher Driver / Pickup Driver
**Action:** The Driver logs into the mobile app, clocks their start reading via the `DailyLog` (`/start`). They see their `PENDING` dashboard assignment and begin updating their journey in real-time.
* **Logistics `assignmentStatus`:** `IN_PROGRESS` (When they actively start working the route).
* **Logistics `transitStatus` flow:** 
  1. `DISPATCHED` (Default)
  2. `EN_ROUTE_TO_FARM`
  3. `ARRIVED_AT_FARM`
  4. `LOADING` (Hand-off to Munshi)
  5. `IN_TRANSIT_TO_DESTINATION`
  6. `ARRIVED_AT_DESTINATION`

---

### Step 5: The Munshi (Loading & Packing)
**Actor:** Munshi (Field Supervisor)
**Action:** While the `transitStatus` is `LOADING`, the Munshi oversees the packing of the boxes at the farm. They generate a `Packing` report and submit it.
* **Packing Status:** Created as `SUBMITTED`
* **Behind the scenes:** The Munshi’s data is sent back to the OM. The OM reviews the weight/box count and approves it.

---

### Step 6: The Driver (Drop-off & Trip Submission)
**Actor:** Eicher Driver / Pickup Driver
**Action:** After arriving at the destination, the Driver unloads. They submit the final `Trip` report (with their ending route, tolls, diesel slip photos, unload slip photos).
* **Logistics `assignmentStatus`:** Changes from `IN_PROGRESS` ➔ `COMPLETED`
* **Enquiry Status:** Remains `ASSIGNED` (Pending OM final approval)

Then they clock out via the `DailyLog` (`/end` shift API).

---

### Step 7: The Operational Manager (Final Approval & Closure)
**Actor:** Operational Manager (OM)
**Action:** The OM reviews the submitted `Trip` and `Packing` reports. If everything matches (Driver's dropped off load vs. Munshi's packed load), the OM officially approves the logistics closure.
* **Logistics `assignmentStatus`:** Changes from `COMPLETED` ➔ `APPROVED`
* **Enquiry Status:** Changes from `ASSIGNED` ➔ `COMPLETED` (and eventually `CLOSED` once accounting settles).

---

### Summary of Status Flow Variables:
**`Enquiry.status`:**
`PENDING` ➔ `SELECTED` ➔ `RATE_FIXED` ➔ `ASSIGNED` ➔ `COMPLETED` ➔ `CLOSED`

**`Logistics.assignmentStatus`:** 
`PENDING` (Assigned by OM) ➔ `IN_PROGRESS` (Driver running) ➔ `COMPLETED` (Driver Drops off) ➔ `APPROVED` (OM verifies).

**`Logistics.transitStatus` (Driver's live map tracking):**
`DISPATCHED` ➔ `EN_ROUTE_TO_FARM` ➔ `ARRIVED_AT_FARM` ➔ `LOADING` ➔ `IN_TRANSIT_TO_DESTINATION` ➔ `ARRIVED_AT_DESTINATION`.
